Abdul’s Logic

This method is not generally accepted as you can see by name it is just a logic that you can use to solve your quadratic without even writing anything. The formula is:
X= -f1/a and X= -f2/a
Where f1 is the first factor, f2 is the second factor and a is the coefficient of x2 Let us try to use it to solve the following question
  1. x2+5x+6=0
  2. 2x2-11x+12=0
  3. x2-13x-48=0
  4. -4x2+12x-9=0
  5. –x2-3x-2=0

Solution1

x2+5x+6=0
Multiply 1*6 =6 and 5. Now think of the two number that add up to 5 and their product is 6
That will be 2 and 3 did you get it?
So f1=2, f2=3 and a=1.
Now X=-f1/a = -2/1 = -2
X = -f2/a = -3/1 = -3

Solution2

2x2-11x+12=0
Multiply 2*12=24 and b=-11. Think of that two number add up to -11 and product be 24 yeah you will get -8 and -3
So f1= -8 and f2=-3 and a=2
Therefore x=-f1/a = --8/2= 8/2 = 4
X=-f2/a = --3/2 = 3/2 = 1.5

Solution3

x2-13x-48=0
Multiply 1*-48=-48 and b=-13.
Think of that two number add up to -13 and product be -48 yeah you will get -16 and 3
So f1= -16 and f2=3 and a=1
Therefore
x=-f1/a = --16/1= 16/1 = 16
X=-f2/a = -3/1 = -3/1 = -3

Solution4

-4x2+12x-9=0
Multiply -4*-9=36 and b=12. Think of that two number add up to 12 and product be 36 yeah you will get 6 and 6
So f1= 6 and f2=6 and a=-4
Therefore
x=-f1/a = -6/-4= -6/-4 = 1.5
X=-f2/a = -6/-4 = -6/-4 = 1.5

Solution 5

–x2-3x-2=0 (thinking of the two numbers that their product gives 2 and addition give -3 we get -1 and -2)
Now it is clear that
F1= 1 and F2= -2 while a=-1
So x=--1/-1 and x=--2/-1
X=1 and x=2
